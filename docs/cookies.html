Cookie Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

Hope Worldwide Uganda is committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency in how we operate.

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Hope Worldwide Uganda ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website, hopewwwug.org.

2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us analyze traffic and improve your experience. We do not use cookies for advertising or selling your data.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Strictly Necessary: Essential for site security and basic functionality.
  • Performance & Analytics: We use Google Analytics to anonymously track visitor numbers and popular pages. This helps us ensure our mission reaches the right audience.
  • Functionality (Third-Party): We embed Google Maps to display our project locations. Google may set cookies to remember your map preferences.

4. Managing Preferences

You can accept or reject cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to delete cookies or block them entirely.

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari

If you have questions about this policy or your data, please contact us:
