Restoring Hope in Uganda

We create, provide, and advocate for health and economic empowerment services for marginalized communities. Our vision is to enable children and youth to attain their full life potential.

1 / 3

What we do

Holistic Health Care

We provide medical camps, maternal care, and fight HIV/AIDS in vulnerable communities. Our focus includes mental health support for youth and saving the lives of mothers and unborn babies.

VIEW HEALTH PROGRAMS →

Empowering Through Education

We build schools, provide scholarships, and ensure the girl child stays in class by providing sanitary pads. We believe education is the key to unlocking potential.

SEE EDUCATION WORK →

Water & Sanitation (WASH)

We construct water sources and promote hygiene in rural areas. Ensuring safe, clean water is vital for preventing disease and improving community well-being.

LEARN ABOUT WASH →

Sustainable Livelihoods

We empower youth and women through vocational skills like tailoring and modern agro-business. We fight poverty by teaching income-generating activities.

OUR LIVELIHOOD PROJECTS →

Legal & Human Rights

We coordinate legal experts to protect the vulnerable, fighting against child labor, corruption, and gender-based violence to ensure justice for all.

HUMAN RIGHTS ADVOCACY →
